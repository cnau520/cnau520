Dipping Powder
As I have mentioned on About me page we will not be using Acrylic powder due to my severely allergic to the fume of monomer liquid.
Benefits of having Dip powder would be

  • It lasts longer than gel and acrylic.
  • Simple and quick application.
  • No UV required.
  • The powder is super pigmented and vibrant.
  • Perfect for nail designs.
  • Odor-free and no mess.
