About Me

Hello there! Thanks for checking out my website. My name is Catherine Nau. I have been doing nails since February of 2018. Making people feel great about themselves have been my passion ever since I started working in this industry. It also gives me a sense of accomplishment. When it comes to nails people tend to think it is a feminine thing and does not associate with men which I completely disagree. Self-care should be essential for everyone, and gender roles should not play a big role in that. So many different services are offered here except acrylic nails since I have allergy to the dust, but we have color dip powder that will be a great substitute for it if you haven’t tried them already, I will highly recommend you that. I take pride in my work and if you get gel color they should last about 2 weeks. If anything happens the first week, please do come back or at least give me a call so that I can fix them for free. Customers’ safety is my priority and I take very serious about providing clean / comfortable environment by changing new pedicure liner after each use, clean the tools with disinfectant solution, and only use “ single-use “ supply to prevent from any cross contamination. Every client’s opinion matters to me because I would not be who I am today without you all. So feel free to give me feedback / suggestions/ comments that will help me and my business. I hope I live up to your expectations and thank you so much for being a part of this journey! I will see you soon!
